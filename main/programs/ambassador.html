Science Expo's Ambassador Program

Science Expo’s (SE) mission is to put a focus on the application and creativity aspects of STEM, providing opportunities to high school students to excel outside of the classroom -- to enrich, empower and explore. Ambassadors are the forerunners of this mission.

 

The Ambassadors Program consists of more than 100 high school students across Alberta, British Columbia and Ontario, representing 40 different cities and regions.

 

The program provides high school students with applicable leadership, community engagement and event planning experience, while fostering relationships within a high-achieving community that spans across Canada (Top 20 Under 20 winners, Next 36 participants, major scholarship/science fair alumna).

 

A Science Expo Ambassador is Someone Who ..

  • Believes in youth empowerment through connections
  • Can commit from June 2015 to June 2016 as a liaison for his/her high school
  • Wants to expand his/her network and can attend an orientation, SE’s annual conference and various social events
  • Believes in collaboration and can exchange hi/her thoughts and ideas through online meetings
  • Wants to be apart of prestigious community
  • Is an event planner at heart and would love to host a Science Expo Seminar
  • Is creative, driven and motivated to run a self-chosen STEM-related initiative
  • Lives on social media and loves sharing cool events with his/her community

Why Should I be a Science Expo Ambassador?

  • Be the face of SE at your school and be featured on our “Meet the Ambassadors” page
  • Receive exclusive access to online workshops, a STEM opportunity database and a network of more than 10,000 individuals
  • Attend our annual Ambassador Summit
  • Receive discounted tickets to SE’s annual conference
  • Start a STEM related initiative with the support of SE
  • Have a say in the development of our vision for external programs
  • Work alongside leadership, national and board members
  • Be featured on any of SE’s social media handles (2,250+ likes on Facebook)
  • Dedicated ambassadors will be provided with a letter of recommendation and volunteer hours
  • Competitive advantage during Science Expo executive recruitment
  • HAVE FUN!
